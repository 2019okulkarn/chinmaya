On May 8, 1916
Balan Menon was born in Ernakulam, Kerala

As a child, he was as normal as any other.
Born into a wealthy family, he went to school and college, where he got a degree in English Literature.

Journalist and Freedom Fighter

Balan started off his career as a journalist and a freedom fighter. He was imprisoned by the British and was later thrown on the streets, where he was found and rescued by a woman passing by. At this time, he was an atheist. He decided to go to Swami Sivananda's ashram in Rishikesh to find out how sannyasi's were 'frauds'.
