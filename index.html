Learn More

  • May 8, 1916

    Balan Menon was born in Ernakulam, Kerala. As a child, he was as normal as any other.

  • 1943

    Balan got postgraduate degrees in both law and literature from Lucknow University.

  • 1947

    Balan went to Rishikesh by the banks of the Ganges, where he visited the Divine Life Society, the ashram of Swami Sivananda.

  • February 25, 1949

    Balan was initiated into sannyasa by Swami Sivananda, who gave him the name Swami Chinmayananda which means "Pure Consciousness"

  • December, 1951

    After 2 years of study under Swami Tapovan Maharaj in the Himalayas, Swami Chinmayananda gave his first lecture in a Ganesh Mandir.

  • August 8, 1953

    Chinmaya Mission is founded to give organization to the work done by Swami Chinmayananda.

  • August 3, 1993

    Swami Chinmayananda suffers from a heart attack and attains Mahasamadhi.

  • May 8, 2015

    Birth centenary celebrations kickoff on Swami Chinmayananda's 99th birthday.

  • Today

    Swami Chinmayananda lives inside of you! Keep smiling!
